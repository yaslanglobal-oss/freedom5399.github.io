核心价值 | Core Values

信息自由获取： 每个人都有权访问知识和信息，不应受到不合理的审查。

Free Access to Information: Everyone has the right to access knowledge and information without unjustified censorship.

隐私与安全： 个人数字隐私应得到尊重，通信加密是基本权利。

Privacy & Security: Personal digital privacy should be respected, and encrypted communication is a fundamental right.

现状与挑战 | Status & Challenges

对抗内容审查： 当今网络世界正面临日益严重的封锁和信息筛选。

Combating Censorship: The online world faces increasing blockades and information filtering.

技术工具： VPN、Tor、去中心化技术是维护自由的必要手段。

Technical Tools: VPNs, Tor, and decentralized technologies are essential means to maintain freedom.
